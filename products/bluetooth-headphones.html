bluetooth-headphones

Bluetooth headphones are wireless audio devices that connect to smartphones, computers, or other compatible devices using Bluetooth technology. They eliminate the need for physical cables, offering greater freedom of movement. Bluetooth headphones are commonly used for listening to music, making phone calls, and gaming. Many models include features like noise cancellation, built-in microphones, and touch controls for convenience and enhanced audio experience.
